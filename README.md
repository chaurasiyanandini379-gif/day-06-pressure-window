# CareerForge Day 6 - The Pressure Window

Product: FlashCart - Drop Incident Command

Role: Incident Command Owner

Core lesson:

> Good engineers do not do everything. They protect the right thing first.

## Why Day 6 Exists

Day 1 taught debugging.

Day 2 taught system reasoning.

Day 3 taught readiness verification.

Day 4 taught ambiguity reduction.

Day 5 taught AI trust judgment.

Day 6 tests prioritization under constrained pressure.

## Scenario

FlashCart is live during a high-traffic product drop.

Checkout instability appears:

- duplicate charges
- inventory mismatch
- webhook lag
- support overload
- refund pressure
- angry customers
- founder revenue pressure
- trust risk

Founder message:

> Revenue is live. Support is exploding. You cannot fix everything. Protect what matters first.
