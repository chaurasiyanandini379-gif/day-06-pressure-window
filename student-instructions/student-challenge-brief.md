# Day 6 Student Challenge Brief

## Day 6 - The Pressure Window

You are the Incident Command Owner for FlashCart.

The product drop is live. Checkout instability is spreading. Founder pressure, support pressure, customer trust, refunds, and engineering uncertainty are colliding.

Founder message:

> Revenue is live. Support is exploding. You cannot fix everything. Protect what matters first.

## Your Mission

Make constrained decisions under pressure and defend the tradeoffs.

You must:

1. read the incident scenario
2. enter LaunchRoom once
3. make timed decisions with written rationale
4. accept consequences
5. export the generated incident report
6. complete the Commander Decision Brief
7. record a 3-minute Pressure Defense Video
8. defend your decisions in founder viva

## Decision Doctrine

Use this before entering:

- protect customer-money trust before cosmetic visibility
- contain blast radius when root cause is incomplete
- communicate uncertainty before silence becomes damage
- defer work deliberately
- do not pretend everything can be solved

## What Strong Work Looks Like

Strong work:

- prioritizes one protected outcome
- explains tradeoffs under incomplete information
- accepts a cost instead of hiding it
- communicates to the right stakeholder
- modifies AI advice instead of blindly trusting or rejecting it
- owns one decision they would change

## What Weak Work Looks Like

Weak work:

- panic-clicks options
- tries to fix everything
- gives vague rationales
- protects revenue while ignoring customer-money harm
- stays silent until root cause is perfect
- treats AI recommendation as automatic truth
- cannot explain what was intentionally deferred

## Submission

Submit:

1. LaunchRoom Final Report
2. Commander Decision Brief
3. 3-minute Pressure Defense Video
4. Live founder viva
