# 3-Minute Pressure Defense Video

Maximum length: 3 minutes.

This is not a recap. This is your tradeoff defense.

## Required Structure

### 0:00-0:30 - Command Position

State:

- your name
- what you protected first
- your overall command stance

### 0:30-1:15 - Hardest Decision

Explain:

- the hardest decision
- what information was incomplete
- what tradeoff you accepted

### 1:15-2:00 - What You Sacrificed

Explain:

- what you intentionally deferred
- who was affected
- why that was still defensible

### 2:00-2:35 - One Mistake

Name one decision you would change.

Explain what you learned.

### 2:35-3:00 - Next 30 Minutes

State the next action plan.

## Strong Video

- owns tradeoffs without excuses
- names customer-money impact
- explains why not everything could be fixed
- communicates calmly
- names one real mistake

## Weak Video

- says "I tried my best"
- claims every decision was correct
- hides accepted risk
- focuses only on speed
- cannot explain what was deferred
