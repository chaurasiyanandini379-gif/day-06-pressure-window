CareerForge Day 6

LaunchRoom

A constrained incident command window for a live FlashCart product drop. Once you enter, the timer does not pause.

Core lesson Engineering is decision-making under pressure.

Scenario

FlashCart is running a major limited product drop. Checkout traffic spikes, duplicate payments appear, webhooks lag, inventory mismatches surface, refunds rise, and stakeholders disagree.

You are the Incident Command Owner. Your job is not to fix everything. Your job is to protect what matters first and own the tradeoffs.

Before You Enter

Rules

  • No pause button. The countdown continues while decisions are pending.
  • Some prompts auto-resolve if you do not decide in time.
  • You only see what has happened so far.
  • Your reasoning, decision latency, and accepted tradeoffs are recorded.

Decision Doctrine

  • Protect customer-money trust before cosmetic visibility.
  • Contain blast radius when root cause is incomplete.
  • Communicate uncertainty before silence becomes damage.
  • Defer work deliberately. Do not pretend everything can be solved.
